Drink Description

A non-alcoholic version of the Moscow Mule. It's an alternative choice for patrons that want something refreshing and heaty. Tastes like its other form, minus the vodka.

Ingredients

  • Ginger beer
  • Lime juice
  • Simple Syrup
  • Fresh mint

Steps

  1. Clap a sprigs-worth of mint together to release the oils and add the leaves to the shaker.
  2. Add 1/2 oz of lime juice, then gently muddle the ingredients together.
  3. Add 1 oz of simple syrup, then ice, and give the shaker a dry shake.
  4. Pour the shaker's contents into a mule cup.
  5. Pour 8 oz of ginger beer and stir.
  6. Garnish with a small sprig of mint and a lime wheel.
  7. Serve!
