Drink Description

A classic pick-me-up on a long night, utilizing simple ingredients. Boldness from the espresso balances out the sweet from the tonic making this a well-rounded drink. Expect a fizzy and clean-cutting mouth feel from every sip.

Ingredients

  • Tonic water
  • Double shot of espresso (1:2 ratio, bean dose to espresso yield)
  • Optional: dried orange wheel or sprig of mint

Steps

  1. Pull a double shot of espresso. Let it cool.
  2. Take a highball glass, add ice, and pour the tonic water.
  3. Once the espresso has cooled, gently pour the shot into the glass of iced tonic water.
  4. Optional: garnish with a dried orange wheel or mint. You may utilize both if you'd like.
  5. Serve!
