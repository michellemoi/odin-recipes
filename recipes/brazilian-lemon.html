Brazilian Lemonade

Home

Drink Description

With the combination of fresh whole lime juice and condensed milk, this drink tastes creamy and sweet with a citrusy tang. A great drink to pair with a summer sunset at the beach.

Ingredients

  • 2 limes (optional: extra lime to slice into wheels)
  • 3 cups of water
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 3 tbsp sweetened condensed milk

Steps

  1. Wash limes and cut the ends. Then, slice them into pieces.
  2. Add cut limes, water, sugar, and sweetened condensed milk into the blender. Blend/pulse until smooth.
  3. Strain blend through a fine mesh strainer.
  4. Take a highball glass and add nugget ice. Pour the lemonade into glass.
  5. Optional: garnish edge of cup with lime wheel.
  6. Serve!
